St Margaret of Scotland Episcopal Church

Dickson Hall

In 2004 St Margaret’s took a momentous step forward with the building of a new, much larger church hall – named the Dickson Hall in honour of the late Nancy Dickson, long-time member, treasurer and secretary of the church.

The hall provides facilities not only for the congregation but also for the wider community, thus giving a further opportunity for outreach and growth.

The Dickson Hall attracts bookings from a wide variety of community groups catering for all ages. Regular weekly bookings include Child Minders, Highland Dancers, Line Dancers, two Yoga classes, Tayport Art Club, China Painters, a Whist Club, and Fife Health Board, as well as children’s parties.
